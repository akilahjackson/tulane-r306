Printing Your Certificate

Your certificate is available to print or download after you successfully complete the course by studying and learning from the course content,  completing the Required Course Survey, and passing the final exam with a score of 80% or higher.

You can access both the Final Exam and the Required Course Survey on the Course Syllabus after you EXIT this portion of the course. To exit this portion of the course, click the link on the top right.

  • When you successfully complete the course, you can immediately print or save a certificate of completion.
  • The certificate prints best in color and with a landscape orientation.  
  • The certificate will provide contact hours and CE (Continuing Education) credits.

After you return to the Course Syllabus, you will see a tab entitled "My Courses" towards the top of the screen. Click on that link and then on the tab entitled "My Transcripts". You will see a list of your completed courses. Click on the "View Certificate" link to be provided access to a printable PDF copy of your Certificate of Completion.

You will continue to have access to online course materials during the time the course is available to you (typically a period of 7 weeks from the first day of enrollment in the course). If you are having trouble printing your certificate, please contact TRDirect@tulane.edu or call 504.988.1342. Your records for course participation, examination grades, and Certificates of Completion are kept for seven (7) years. You may contact trdirect@tulane.edu for a transcript of your completed courses.
