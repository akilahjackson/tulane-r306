Final Exam Instructions

After reviewing the course summary, you are ready to demonstrate how much you have learned in this course by completing the Final Exam. To access the exam, click EXIT (top right) to close this window and locate the FINAL EXAM link on the Course Syllabus.

Final Exam:

  • Please use course resources to assist you in passing the Exam. You may view or print notes and course materials for reference.
  • Remember, you need to achieve a passing score of at least 80% on the Exam. 
  • Multiple choice or True/False Exam questions will be presented. Once you have completed the exam, click the submit button at the bottom of the screen to receive your score.

You have been provided with unlimited attempts for passing the Exam, but you should thoroughly review the course materials before trying again.

Once you have launched the Final Exam, if you want to return to the course material without completing the Exam, exit the exam and relaunch the course content. You can save your current answers without submitting them for a score before exiting the exam.

Additional instructions will be available immediately preceding the Exam.
